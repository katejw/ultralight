Describe the room you're sitting with

The room is very open, the windows are across two walls, spanning most of the wall. I can feel the breeze, and there is an abundance of light coming inside. I can hear the cars passing by. Outside are mostly houses and stores, giving the sense that I am almost off campus. The table and the chairs are in the middle of the room, around a large table, fueling the sense of openness that penetrates throughout. It is easy to think of this room being a space for art from the pile of coins on the table to the white paper taped onto the desk, and various projects in progress behind. The variation of the chairs from its shape to its height adds to the sense of freedom evoked by the room. The abundance of white colors in the room adds to this. Cohesively, this sense of freedom and openness come together to give conviction that the space is meant for creativity and creation.
